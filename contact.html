DPC: Contact

Authors

The DPC screening rules were developed by Jie Wang and Jieping Ye, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Arizona State University, Tempe, AZ, USA.

The DPC package was written by Jie Wang.

The solver used by DPC is from the SLEP package written by Jun Liu (the DPC screening rules can be integrated with any existing solvers).

Maintainer

Jie Wang is the developer for the DPC package. Please contact Jie for any comments, bugs, or problems integrating DPC with other solvers.
